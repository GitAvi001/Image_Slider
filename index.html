Xiaomi

MIUI: Most Xiaomi phones run on MIUI, the company's custom Android-based operating system.
MIUI offers a unique user experience with customizable themes, advanced customization options, and regular updates.

Apple

iOS features a clean and intuitive user interface characterized by its sleek design, fluid animations, and consistent visual elements.
It employs a grid of app icons on the home screen and a dock for quick access to frequently used apps.
iOS seamlessly integrates with other Apple devices and services through features like AirDrop, Handoff, iCloud, iMessage, and Continuity. This allows for a consistent user experience across devices.
